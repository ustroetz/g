Otion Front Studio is a performance space in Brooklyn co-run by Monica Mirabile, Sigrid Lauren, Sarah Kinlaw, Kathleen Dycaico & Gina Chiappetta.

 

OFS serves as a physical platform for the exploration of movement through contemporary means of processing information. As we delve deeper into constantly changing paradigms, Otion Front aims to balance physicality and technologically induced ontologies. We offer periodic workshops in dance and movement as well as a residency program dedicated to exploring movement art. Otion Front also hosts monthly performance and dance art video screening events.
